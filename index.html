    Hi, I’m Saad, a passionate game developer who thrives on crafting immersive experiences. My journey into coding wasn’t love at first sight—back in my computer science days, I found algorithms dry and uninspiring. But everything changed when I discovered game development. Suddenly, physics wasn’t just equations—it was ragdolls flying gloriously after a grenade blast. Math became enemy AI pathfinding that made me yell, “Oh, you sneaky bot!” at my screen. Even art clicked—I finally understood why shading a 3D model feels like giving it a soul.

    Now, I’m the kind of developer who gets genuinely hyped about designing clean, scalable game architectures, tackling “impossible” bugs with a “hold my energy drink” mindset, and obsessing over game feel—because perfecting a single jump animation’s squash and stretch is totally worth it. I love falling into flow states where hours disappear in minutes, every line of code feels like adding brushstrokes to a masterpiece, and my heart sparks with joy when players ask, “Wait, how did you do that?!”

    The best part? I’m still that kid who dreams of making games so engaging that players forget to check their phones. I thrive in collaborative environments where artists, designers, and developers come together to create magic. Every project is a playground for wild ideas, and I believe that even hyper-casual games deserve soul. Let’s build something that doesn’t just entertain but leaves players with that electric, “I NEED to show this to someone” feeling.
